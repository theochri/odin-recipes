Apple Pie

This popular pie recipe makes a caramelized lattice crust filled with sweet, buttery apple slices for the perfect fall or holiday dessert!

Let's dig into it!

Ingredients

    For the dough
  • 250g butter,cold,cut into cubes
  • 450g all-purpose flour
  • 50g icing sugar
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 2 eggs,medium
  • 1 tablespoon water
    For the filling
  • 3 red apples
  • 3 Granny Smith apples
  • lemon juice, from 1/2 lemon
  • 50g digestive cookies,crushed
    To assemble
  • 50g butter, for the tart pan
  • 1 egg yolk, from a medium egg, diluted with 1 tablespoon of water
  • 50g granulated sugar, for sprinkling

    • Steps

      For the dough
    1. Add the butter, flour, icing sugar, and salt into a large bowl, and knead them with your hands for 1 minute, until the butter is completely dissolved and the mixture looks like wet sand. Ideally, you should wear gloves to prevent the heat of your hands from melting the butter.
    2. Add the eggs and the water, and mix well until you have a smooth, pliable dough.
    3. Wrap the dough well in plastic wrap.
    4. Refrigerate the dough, for 1-2 hours, until it is cool and set.
      For the apples
    1. Place a large frying pan over high heat and allow it to heat up well.
    2. Peel the apples and cut them into medium-sized slices.
    3. Add the sugar to the pan and allow it to caramelize.
    4. Once it is caramelized, add the apples and stir well with a serving spoon.
    5. Add the lemon juice and stir with the spoon.
    6. Add the cinnamon, lower the heat to medium, and simmer the apples for 5-10 minutes until they are tender.
    7. Remove the pan from the heat and transfer the caramelized apples to a bowl.
    8. Add the crushed cookies and mix well with a spoon.
      To assemble
    1. Preheat the oven to 170°C (340° F) set to fan.
    2. Remove the dough from the refrigerator and place it on your worktop.
    3. Cut the dough into two pieces.
    4. If the room is warm, place 2 parchment paper pieces on your worktop and roll out the dough between them, so that the butter inside the dough does not heat up.
    5. Use a rolling pin to roll out the first dough piece into a thin, round sheet. If needed, dust it with more flour and cover it with a piece of parchment paper to roll it out more easily.
    6. Butter a 28 cm round springform tart pan and spread the round dough sheet in it.
    7. Trim the overhanging edges and spread the apple filling over the dough.
    8. Use the rolling pin to roll out the second dough piece into a thin sheet and cut it into long strips.
    9. Place the dough strips over the apple filling, in a zig-zag pattern, and trim the overhanging edges.
    10. Use a pastry brush to spread the egg wash over the apple pie and sprinkle it with the sugar, so it becomes nicely golden during baking.
    11. Transfer the tart pan to the oven and bake the apple pie for 50-60 minutes.
    12. Remove the tart pan from the oven and take the apple pie out of the pan.
    13. Cut the apple pie into pieces and serve with icing sugar and mint leaves.
